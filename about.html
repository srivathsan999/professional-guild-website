About The Guild

Celebrating 40 years of professional excellence and community building.

Who We Are

The Professional Guild is the premier body representing specialists in our industry. From our humble beginnings in 1985, we have grown into a global network of over 15,000 members dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of practice.

We work tirelessly with policymakers, educational institutions, and industry leaders to ensure that our profession remains relevant, ethical, and respected.

Our History

1985
