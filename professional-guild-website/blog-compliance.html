Regulatory Updates Q4: Navigating the New Landscape

By Compliance Committee • Oct 10, 2025

As we approach the end of the final quarter of 2025, the Professional Guild is releasing a comprehensive update on the regulatory changes affecting our industry. These shifts are designed to increase transparency and protect both practitioners and the public.

Digital Privacy Mandates

The primary focus of this quarter's updates revolves around data sovereignty and digital privacy. New protocols require all members to undergo a mandatory security audit of their client-facing digital systems by the end of the year.

Code of Conduct Revisions

Section 4.2 of the Guild's Code of Conduct has been revised to explicitly address automated client interactions. It is now a requirement that any automated system—from chatbots to algorithmic processing—must be clearly disclosed to the end-user.

The Guild remains committed to supporting our members through these transitions. Detailed webinars explaining the specific implementation steps will be held throughout November.
